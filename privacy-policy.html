2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information that is necessary to provide and operate the App. We never collect more than we need, and we never collect data for advertising or commercial profiling.

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address — used to authenticate your account and send service-related communications.
  • Full name — displayed within your organization so other members can identify who reported an issue or completed a job.
  • Phone number (optional) — provided only if you choose to add it for contact purposes within your organization.

2.2 Organization Information

  • Company or workshop name — used to associate users with the correct organization for access control.

2.3 Vehicle and Operational Data

  • Vehicle information: VIN (Vehicle Identification Number), license plate, make, model, year, fuel type, and unit number.
  • Operational records: issue descriptions, job notes, repair status updates, and scheduling dates.

2.4 Media (Photos)

  • Issue and repair photos — uploaded by users to document truck issues and completed repairs. Stored in encrypted Supabase Storage.

2.5 Technical Data

  • Push notification tokens — generated by your device's operating system; required to deliver job and status notifications. Tokens are cleared on sign-out and overwritten when you sign in on a new device.
  • Crash and performance data — collected via Sentry to detect and resolve bugs. May include device model, OS version, and a stack trace of where the App failed. We do not associate crash reports with personal identifiers for marketing purposes.

2.6 What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your geographic location.
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies (Hauler is a mobile app).
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or perform cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not sell or rent personal data to anyone.
  • We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • We do not perform automated decision-making or profiling.

4. How and Why We Use Your Information

  • Authentication and account management — your email and (hashed) password let you securely sign in via our authentication provider, Supabase.
  • Service operation — your name and organization membership let other authorized users see who reported an issue, who is assigned to a job, and who completed a repair.
  • Fleet and repair workflow — vehicle records, issue reports, job notes, and photos let trucking companies and workshops coordinate repairs.
  • Notifications — push notification tokens let us deliver real-time updates about job assignments, status changes, and scheduling.
  • Service quality and reliability — crash and performance data lets us identify and resolve bugs and outages.
  • Compliance and legal obligations — we may process data when required to comply with applicable law or to defend our legal rights.

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or to make automated decisions that produce legal or significant effects.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above:

Data CategoryRetention Period
Account data (email, name, phone, organization membership) Retained until you delete your account from within the App.
Vehicle and operational data (issues, jobs, notes) Retained until the associated company deletes the truck or the account is deleted.
Photos (issue and repair documentation) Retained until the associated issue or job is deleted.
Crash and performance data Approximately 90 days (Sentry's default retention period).

When you delete your account through the in-app "Delete my account" option, we run an automated procedure that removes your profile and unlinks you from any organizations.

8. Your Rights Under the GDPR

If you are in the EEA, the UK, or another jurisdiction with comparable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and information about how we process it.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — you may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data. Most account fields can be edited directly in the App.
  • Right to erasure / "right to be forgotten" (Art. 17) — you may request deletion of your personal data. You can self-serve this by using "Delete my account" in the App, or by emailing us.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18) — you may ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances (for example, while we verify the accuracy of your data).
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — you may request a copy of the personal data you provided to us in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (e.g., JSON or CSV).
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — you may object to processing carried out on the basis of our legitimate interest (such as crash and performance monitoring). When you object, we will stop processing unless we have compelling legitimate grounds that override your interests.

How to exercise your rights

Email alan98.aj@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, describing the right you would like to exercise. We will respond within 30 days (this period may be extended by up to two further months for complex or numerous requests, in which case we will inform you).

Exercising your rights is free of charge. We may ask you to provide reasonable information to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

11. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. These include:

  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit between your device and our servers.
  • Encrypted storage of authentication tokens on your device.
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies in our database to enforce strict isolation between organizations.
  • Hashed and salted passwords managed by Supabase Authentication.
  • Periodic review of access controls and sub-processor security posture.

No system can be guaranteed 100% secure. If we become aware of a personal data breach affecting your information, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authorities as required by law.

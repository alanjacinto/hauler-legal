Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Hauler ("the App") is a fleet management and workshop dispatch application developed by Alan Jacinto ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • Account information: Your email address and full name, provided during registration. These are used for authentication and identification within your organization.
  • Operational data: Information you enter while using the App, including truck details, issue reports, job assignments, and mechanic notes. This data belongs to your organization and is shared only with members of the same organization.
  • Crash and performance data: Anonymous technical data collected through Sentry to identify and fix bugs. This data is not linked to your identity and includes no personal information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.
  • We do not collect browsing or search history.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: Your email and password are used to securely log you into the App via Supabase, our authentication provider.
  • App functionality: Your name is displayed to other members of your organization so they can identify who reported an issue, who is assigned to a job, or who manages a workshop.
  • Bug fixing and reliability: Crash and performance data helps us identify and resolve technical issues to improve the App's stability.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security (RLS) policies that ensure strict data isolation between organizations. Company A cannot access Company B's data, and Workshop A cannot access Workshop B's data. Authentication tokens are encrypted on your device using AES-256 encryption.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with members of your own organization as required by the App's functionality (for example, a workshop manager can see the trucks of companies linked to their workshop). We do not share your data with any third party for advertising, analytics beyond crash reporting, or any other purpose.

The only third-party service that receives data from the App is Sentry (for crash and performance monitoring). Sentry receives anonymous technical data that is not linked to your identity.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Operational data (issues, jobs, truck records) is retained for as long as your organization uses the App, even if individual users leave, because this data belongs to the organization.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App by navigating to Account Settings and selecting "Delete my account." Upon deletion:

  • Your profile and authentication credentials are permanently deleted.
  • Your organization memberships are removed.
  • Issues you reported and jobs you were assigned to are preserved for your organization's records, but your name is removed from them.

Account deletion is immediate and irreversible.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for professional fleet management use and is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through the App or by other appropriate means. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated.
